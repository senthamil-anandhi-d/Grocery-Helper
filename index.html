To sync your history across devices:

  1. Create a project at Firebase Console.
  2. Add a **Web App** and copy the firebaseConfig values.
  3. Paste them into the top of app.js.
  4. In **Authentication**, enable "Email/Password".
History will still save locally even without cloud setup.
